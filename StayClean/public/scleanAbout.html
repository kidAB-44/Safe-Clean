Who We Are

  • Stay Clean is the leading provider of residential and commercial waste management services in Uganda.

  • Our purpose is to empower communities to keep the environments they live in healthy and happy.

  • Our 350 employees serve around 250,000 households, in 20 major towns and cities across Uganda.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission at Safe Clean is to provide reliable automated waste management services to communities. Our team goes over and above to provide an exceptional service. Our solutions can be tailored to meet specific community needs so if you don't see what you are looking for, call us to learn more.
